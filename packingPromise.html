Our Packing Promise

As a company who firmly believe in the ethos of reduce-reuse-recycle all our packaging is biodegradable and where possible is reused from packaging we receive.
This includes using shredded boxes and paper rather than packing peanuts.
All our packaging has been checked for suitability and all packages are insured for your piece of mind.

*Next working day delivery if ordered before 1pm on the previous working day
