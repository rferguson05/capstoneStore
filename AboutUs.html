About Us

We are a small family run business based outside Edinburgh. At the moment we stock pre-loved gaming peripherals. We use our expertise to scour online market places and auction sites to find the best deal possible for our customers. We then take these devices and, where appropriate and required, clean them and provide a full check. We look for things like stick drift and sticky buttons so that you don't lose out in those high stakes online moments.
